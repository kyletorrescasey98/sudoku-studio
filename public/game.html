Sudoku
Classic 9×9 Number Placement Puzzle
1️⃣
RowsEach of the 9 rows must contain the digits 1–9 with no repetition.
2️⃣
ColumnsEach of the 9 columns must contain the digits 1–9 with no repetition.
3️⃣
BoxesEach of the nine 3×3 boxes must contain the digits 1–9 with no repetition.
🔓
Given CluesGrey digits are pre-filled and cannot be changed.
✏️
Corner MarksSmall amber digits in cell corners — track possible candidates.
⊙
Centre MarksPurple digits centred in the cell — mark cells belonging to a group.
🎯
ObjectiveFill every empty cell so all rows, columns, and boxes are complete.
🔢
Unique SolutionEvery generated puzzle has exactly one valid solution.
